Beef Wellington

The Beef Wellington is a fantastic dish made of beef tenderloin surrounded by puff pastry

It also contains mushroom duxelle, parma ham. Which is all wrapped together and baked.

Ingredients

  • Beef Tenderloin
  • Mushroom Duxelle
  • Puff Pastry
  • Master Skills

Steps

  1. Season beef in salt/pepper
  2. Sear beef, 1 min each side
  3. Brush Dijon mustard on beef
  4. Layer puff pastry, add duxelle and prosciutto
  5. Roll beef in pastry
  6. Baste pastry in egg wash
  7. Bake in oven at 425F for 18-25 min
  8. Enjoy!
