Oats and Fruit

This dish is great for breakfast or as a preworkout. The oatmeal and fruit is a great source of healthy carbs and vitamins

Ingredients

  • Instant oats
  • Whole milk
  • Frozen fruit(mixed berry)

Steps

  1. Pour oats in bowl
  2. Add whole milk
  3. Add fruit
  4. Put in microwave for 3 1/2 minutes
  5. Enjoy!
